Why I Started This Project ?

After completing my 10th grade, I found myself at a crossroads. While my parents were loving and supportive, they lacked the educational background to guide me toward the right career path. As a result, my future was often shaped by the opinions of neighbors and well-meaning but uninformed advice. This lack of direction left me feeling confused and uncertain about my choices. It made me realize just how crucial it is for students to have access to proper guidance and resources when shaping their careers.
